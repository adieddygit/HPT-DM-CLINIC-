About Us

At DORNUEL HEALTH CONSULT, we believe that wellbeing is the foundation of success. Our mission is to empower individuals to lead healthier, happier lives by providing the resources, tools, and support they need to prioritize their health.

Our Vision

We envision a world where health and wellbeing are at the core of everyone’s daily life. Whether it's through professional advice, personalized plans, or simply fostering a community of support, we are committed to helping people achieve their personal health goals.

Our Mission

We aim to provide accessible, reliable, and professional services that improve overall health and wellbeing of peaple living with Hypertension and Diabetes. By leveraging modern technologies, scientific research, and experienced professionals, we make health and wellness a top priority for all.

Our Core Values

  • Commitment to Health: Everything we do revolves around the importance of health.
  • Compassion: We care deeply about our users and always put their needs first.
  • Innovation: We embrace new ideas, technologies, and research to provide cutting-edge services.
  • Integrity: We value transparency, honesty, and trust in every interaction.

What We Offer

  • Personalized Wellness Plans: Tailored health and wellness plans designed specifically for you.
  • Expert Advice: Access to a network of healthcare professionals who are ready to guide you every step of the way.
  • Resources: Articles, guides, and tips to help you make informed decisions about your health.
  • Community Support: Join a community of like-minded individuals who share the goal of achieving health lives.

Our Journey

Founded in 2021, DORNUEL HEALTH CONSULT started with a simple goal: to bridge the gap between health education and everyday wellness. Over the years, we’ve grown into a trusted platform used by thousands to improve their lifestyle through proper health management and professional guidance.
